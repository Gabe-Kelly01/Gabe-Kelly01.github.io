About Me

Hi! My name is Gabe, and I recently graduated from Whitman College with a degree in Computer Science. While I was an undergraduate I interned at the Ingeniux Corporation and Hudson's Bay Company, where I gained experience with software and cloud engineering. In my senior year, I was a part of a team performing research on the applications of machine learning in robot automated silver-nanocrystal synthesis. In addition to that I also worked as a peer tutor, helped craft student safety policy as a represenatative on Whitman's Dean's Advisory Commitee, and served a term as President of the Sigma Chi fraternity. I hope to eventually pursue a PhD in computer science.

I am motivated first and foremost by my passion for computer science. I truly enjoy programming and technical problem solving. At the same time, I have a strong sense of ethics and social responsibility. My goal is to find a company where I can leverage my talents to drive company growth and postive social impacts.
